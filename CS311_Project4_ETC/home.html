Great Composers

Introduction

This website is dedicated to introducing several key classical composers in music history: Bizet, Mozart, Puccini, Verdi and Wagner. These composers have produced some amazing timeless masterpieces. They all lived in either the 18th or 19th centuries. There works have long outlived their physical bodies. Each page will introduce you to one of the composers, provide a brief biography, a list of some of their most famous works, and an image of that composer. If you click on the image you can hear some of their work!
